About LimbLink

More Than Prosthetics.
We Help People Move Forward.

LimbLink was founded on the belief that mobility changes lives. Our mission is to simplify the connection between patients and trusted prosthetic care through compassionate support, education, and referral coordination.

As a disabled veteran-owned company, we believe every individual deserves dignity, support, and the opportunity to reclaim independence and confidence.
